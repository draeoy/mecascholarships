Club Goals

Club Goals

What our scholarship club aims to accomplish this year.

  • Help students find and apply to merit and need-based scholarships.
  • Centralize announcements about fly-ins and summer programs.
  • Offer workshops: essays, resumes, recommendation strategies.
  • Host practice interviews and mock scholarship committee panels.
  • Pair juniors with senior mentors for application and timeline help.
  • Maintain a curated resources page with official scholarship links.
  • Organize group trips to campus fly-ins and college visits.
  • Provide hands-on help with online application portals and forms.
